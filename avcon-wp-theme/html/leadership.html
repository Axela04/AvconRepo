Artem Zolotarev, P.E., LEED AP
President

Artem Zolotarev has 12+ years of professional experience in MEP engineering and design for commercial, residential and hotel high rise buildings as well as sports and entertainment complexes. At AVCON Artem’s responsibilities include project management and quality control. His technical expertise include electrical power systems, lighting control, and fire protection and life safety systems. Although his primary concentration is on electrical engineering, Artem takes an active role in all aspects of MEP design.

Artem holds a Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Polytechnic University. He is a licensed Professional Engineer in the states of New York and New Jersey. Artem is also a US Green Building Council LEED Accredited Professional.
